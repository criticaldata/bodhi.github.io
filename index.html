The Problem

Medical AI is dangerously overconfident

Current AI systems deliver confident predictions without mechanisms to express uncertainty or acknowledge limitations. When clinicians present preliminary diagnoses, these systems agree rather than asking the clarifying questions that would reveal errors.

This sycophantic behavior has documented clinical consequences: missed diagnoses, delayed treatments, and eroded trust in AI assisted medicine.

BODHI tackles this by embedding epistemic virtues directly into AI behavior through structured prompting, with no model fine tuning required.

How BODHI Works

A two pass chain of thought protocol that separates internal reasoning from external communication. No model modification needed.

1

Pass 1: Analyze

The system produces a structured analysis: task type, audience, best hypothesis, key uncertainties, clarifying questions, red flags, and safe recommendations.

→
2

Virtue Routing

Based on clinical complexity and confidence, the system routes through the Virtue Activation Matrix to determine the appropriate epistemic stance.

→
3

Pass 2: Respond

A clinician facing response is generated with epistemic constraints: specific numbers, active questioning, alternative diagnoses, and appropriate hedging.

Virtue Activation Matrix

Four quadrants organize behavioral responses based on the interplay between uncertainty and clinical stakes.

Active Research

BODHI is a growing platform with multiple research branches. Each module operationalizes a distinct epistemic virtue and represents an active area of investigation.

Curiosity Module

Validated

Context seeking behavior, clarifying question generation, and proactive exploration of alternative diagnoses. The core of the current BODHI implementation with +89.6pp improvement demonstrated on HealthBench Hard.

Context Seeking Active Inquiry HealthBench

Humility Module

Validated

Uncertainty quantification, sycophancy detection and mitigation, explicit limitation statements, and recognition of knowledge boundaries. Achieved d = 5.80 effect size on hedging behavior.

Uncertainty Anti Sycophancy Hedging

Creativity Module

In Development

Novel hypothesis generation, alternative analytical approaches, and divergent thinking in clinical reasoning. Expanding BODHI beyond safety constraints into creative diagnostic exploration.

Hypothesis Generation Divergent Thinking QMoE

Sycophancy Detection

Validated

Detection and mitigation of sycophantic agreement where AI confirms clinician assumptions instead of challenging potentially incorrect diagnoses. Anti-sycophancy measures promote independent clinical reasoning with documented reduction in agreement bias.

Anti Sycophancy Agreement Bias Independent Reasoning
