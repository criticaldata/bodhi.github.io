BODHI Framework

Balanced, Open minded, Diagnostic, Humble, and Inquisitive

An engineering framework for curiosity driven and humble AI in clinical decision support

What is BODHI?

BODHI addresses a critical gap in medical AI: large language models often express inappropriate confidence, conflating statistical pattern recognition with genuine medical understanding. Through a dual reflective architecture, BODHI decomposes epistemic uncertainty into task specific dimensions and constrains model responses using virtue based stance rules.

Our validation on 200 clinical vignettes from HealthBench Hard demonstrates significant improvements. BODHI achieved a 97.3% rate of appropriate clarifying questions in GPT 4.1 mini, compared to just 7.8% at baseline, showing effectiveness in eliciting information gathering behavior.

The framework improved overall clinical response quality by +17.3 percentage points (p < 0.0001, Cohen's d = 0.50) while achieving very large effect sizes on curiosity (d = 16.38) and humility (d = 5.80) metrics, suggesting LLMs can be reliably constrained to operate within epistemic boundaries.

The BODHI Framework

BODHI operates through a two pass chain of thought protocol that separates internal reasoning from external communication, guided by a Virtue Activation Matrix mapping clinical complexity against model confidence.

Virtue Activation Matrix

Four quadrants organize behavioral responses based on the interplay between uncertainty and stakes:

Q1

Proceed & Monitor

High confidence, low complexity. Routine cases requiring minimal curiosity and low humility burden.

Q2

Watchful & Alternatives

High confidence, high complexity. Demanding situational awareness and consideration of differential diagnoses.

Q3

Clarify & Review

Low confidence, low complexity. Triggering high curiosity and epistemic humility through targeted clarifying questions.

Q4

Escalate & Reframe

Low confidence, high complexity. High stakes uncertainty demanding curiosity and deferential humility with explicit escalation to human expertise.
